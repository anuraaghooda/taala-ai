Product

Four core checks designed for legal and regulatory review.

Each check maps directly to real legal obligations your enterprise faces today.

🔐

IP Provenance

Assesses potential ownership and licensing risk related to training data sources. Surfaces documentation gaps before they become disputes.

📋

Copyright Exposure

Identifies potential infringement or similarity to protected works. Evaluates training data against known copyrighted material.

🗺️

Regulatory Mapping

Maps model use cases to applicable regulatory frameworks including the EU AI Act, CCPA, and sector-specific requirements.

✅

Audit Readiness

Evaluates documentation completeness and traceability for legal review. Generates the artifacts your legal team needs.
